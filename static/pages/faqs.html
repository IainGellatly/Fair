How do I install the Enjoy the Fair App?
Tap the install icon in the upper right corner.

If you are on Android you just need to confirm the install and then look for the Fair icon in your app menu to start.

If you are on Apple, follow the instruction popup, add the app to your home screen and then look for the Fair icon in your app menu to start.
Is the Enjoy the Fair App free?
The Enjoy the Fair App is free and also completely ad-free. There is no cost to install and use the app. Use the app to explore everything the Wayne County Fair has to offer.
Do I have to install the app or can I just use it through my browser?
You do not have to install the app. However, some features will work much better if you install it. We recommend at least bookmarking through a browser. You can remove the app at any time.
How do I remove the Enjoy the Fair App?
Tap and hold the Fair icon and drag it to the uninstall bar on your phone screen just like any other app.
I have questions about the Enjoy the Fair App. Where can I get help?
Visit the Fair Information booth at the south end of Floral Hall for assistance. Please also complete the survey in the app to provide any feedback you have. We love ideas and input from our visitors.
Are there ATMs on the fairgrounds?
Yes. ATMs are available at multiple locations throughout the fairgrounds. Please visit the Map page in this app for the exact ATM locations.
Can I get a refund on tickets?
All ticket sales are final. Refunds are not available except in limited circumstances. For details, please visit waynecountyfair.org.
What happens if it rains?
The fair is open rain or shine. Some events may be delayed or rescheduled due to weather or cancelled due to extreme conditions. Watch the Today Events for updates.
What should I do in a weather emergency?
Follow announcements over the fair loudspeakers and directions from staff. Seek shelter in designated buildings or safe areas.
Is smoking allowed?
Smoking and vaping are only permitted in designated areas. Please follow posted signs and be respectful of others.
Are pets allowed?
No pets are allowed on the fairgrounds. Registered service animals are permitted, but must be registered at the Fair Main Office.
Where is First Aid located?
First Aid is available on-site during all fair hours near the north end of the Fair. See the First Aid or Map sections of this app for location details.
Where are restrooms located?
Restrooms are available throughout the fairgrounds. See the Facilities or Map sections of this app for locations.
Is there a Lost & Found?
Yes. Lost and Found is available at the Fair Information Booth on the south side of Floral Hall. If you lose an item please report it as soon as possible.
What if someone is causing a disturbance?
Please notify fair staff or security immediately. Disruptive behavior will not be tolerated and may result in removal from the fair.
Are outside food or drinks allowed?
Outside food and beverages are not permitted, except for medical or dietary restriction needs. Visit the Food Court for a wide range of food and drink vendors.
Are strollers and wagons allowed?
Yes. Strollers and wagons are allowed, but please be mindful of other visitors in crowded areas.
Can I walk the fairgrounds with alcohol?
Yes, you may if you are of legal age and with a proof of age wrist strap on at all times. You may not leave the fairgrounds with alcohol.
Is parking available?
Yes. Free parking is available on the fairgrounds. The parking entrance is at the south end of the fairgrounds on East Foster Street. Follow posted signs and staff directions when arriving. Handicap parking is also available.
Do vendors accept credit cards?
Many vendors accept credit/debit cards, but not all. It’s recommended to bring some cash. There are also several ATMs at the Fair.
How can I become a vendor or exhibitor?
Vendor applications and information are available at: waynecountyfair.org
What are the animal health requirements?
Visit waynecountyfair.org for more information.
Can I become a volunteer at the Fair?
Yes! We would love the help. The fair offers many volunteer roles, including maintenance, visitor relations, judging, education and more. Visit waynecountyfair.org for more information and an online application form.
Is WiFi available?
Public WiFi at the Fair is limited. Cellular service is generally better for mobile access.
Are bags allowed?
Yes, but all bags may be subject to inspection for safety.
