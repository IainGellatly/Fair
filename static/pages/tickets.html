Fair Entrance Tickets

Booster Pass (good for the week), discounted pre-sale Daily Admission and All Access Pass are available.

Buy tickets at the gate or online.
Fair Special Event Tickets

Select Fair events require an additional ticket, including:

  • Demolition Derby
  • Wine Tasting
  • Empire State Pullers Tractor Pull
  • Figure 8 Racing
  • Beat the Beast Bull Riders Tour Rodeo
  • Black Cat Hell Driver Stunt Show
Buy tickets at the gate on online.
