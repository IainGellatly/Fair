Restrooms and Showers

Restrooms are located near the Main Gate on the north side of the Fair, inside the Cattle Barn on the east side of the Fair and near the Grandstand on the west side of the Fair. See the Map for exact locations.

Porta potties are also conveniently located throughout the fairgrounds.

Restrooms are cleaned hourly. Please report problems at the Fair Information Booth at the south side of Floral Hall or by calling 315-597-5372.
