For life-threatening emergencies call 911 immediately

For all other medical needs and first aid assistance, our on-site Medical Station is open during normal fair hours and is staffed by trained medical professionals. Please note medical personnel cannot administer medications.

The Medical Station is located on the north side of the main restrooms, adjacent to Floral Hall. Check the Map for exact location.

If you are unable to reach the Medical Station, please notify the nearest fair staff member, who will assist you in obtaining medical support.
